Thoughts on Firebase...

Learning about Firebase was a good introduction to what is possible when you have the option to store and read data from and to a remote location. With Firebase, writing your own chat program or application that maintains your personal shopping list becomes dead simple. Furthermore, being introduced to the possibilities of web APIs makes programming a much more interesting pastime as it opens up the possibility to interact with other content apart from one's own. Last but not least, I really like the idea that I can add more quotes to my Firebase object and seconds later they can be seen by someone who just happens to be looking at this page.
