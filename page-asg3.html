Thoughts on d3...

d3 is beautiful and it fascinated me from the start. Binding data to objects and letting that data define certain attributes of those objects opens up a vast landscape of possibilities. Realising that these objects don't necessarily have to be SVGs is very exciting and makes me picture websites whose content defines how and where the content should be displayed. I also wonder about possible real-time applications, where event handlers listen for changes in data and/or content and trigger updates of graphs and layout on the fly...
